THE EUROPEAN COMMISSION,

Having regard to the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union,

Having regard to Regulation (EC) No 1221/2009 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 25 November 2009 on the voluntary participation by organisations in a Community eco-management and audit scheme (EMAS), repealing Regulation (EC) No 761/2001 and Commission Decisions 2001/681/EC and 2006/193/EC, and in particular Article 46(5) thereof,

Whereas:

Companies and other organisations should receive additional information and guidance about the steps needed to participate in EMAS,

HAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION:

Article 1

To provide additional information clarifying the steps needed to participate in EMAS, the Commission adopts this user guide.

Article 2

This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
